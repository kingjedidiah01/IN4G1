Prof.(Arc) Ekundayo Adeyinka Adeyemi

The first Architecture Professor in Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa

Some Notable Achievement and Timeline:

  • 1937 - Born in Iyin-Ekiti, Ekiti State, Nigeria.
  • 1956 - Completes Secondary School at Christ's Schools, Ado-Ekiti.
  • 1963 - Obtained Degree in Architecture from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria.
  • 1965 - bagged Masters Degree from Columbia University.
  • 1965 - Got married to his wife, Mrs. Beatrice Adeyemi(n√©e Olofin).
  • 1969 - started his lecturing career at Ahmadu Bello University.
  • 1973- He bagged another Master's in urban planning from New York University
  • 1974 - His PhD thesis was on Kaduna Capital Territory and Metropolitan Lagos: An Analysis of Administrative and Institutional Framework for Urban land Planning and Development and was completed in the same institution
  • 1975 - Was made a professor in the same University
  • 1988 - Appointed as Deputy Vice Chancellor of Federal University of Technology Akure
  • 1999 - Was selected as Ag. Vice Chancellor of the school.
  • 2002 - He Retired in the same University
  • 2004 - He came out of retirement to become the dean of College of Science and Technology and distinguished professor in Covenant University, Ota.
  • 2022 - dies at the age of 84.
